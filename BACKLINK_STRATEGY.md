## Red Flags to Avoid

### Don't Do:
1. **Buy backlinks** - Google penalty risk
2. **Use link farms** - Will hurt, not help
3. **Exact match anchor text overuse** - Looks spammy
4. **Reciprocal link schemes** - Low value
5. **Comment spam** - Waste of time
6. **Low-quality directories** - Can hurt more than help
7. **Automated link building tools** - High risk

### Quality Over Quantity:
- 10 high-DA, relevant backlinks > 100 low-quality links
- Focus on DR 40+ websites
- Prioritize industry relevance
- Natural anchor text distribution
- Editorial links (within content) > footer/sidebar links
